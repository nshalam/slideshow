The Prompt:

Step into the roles of your teachers and envision a piece of technology that would streamline their teaching process and make teaching easier. As students yourselves, consider what kind of technology would best help your learning in a more engaging and effective manner. Imagine a world where education is more accessible to everyone, and think about how technology can bridge that gap. All these innovations fall under the category of ed-tech, short for educational technology, and it is one of the fastest growing industries in the world. Your challenge is to devise an ed-tech solution that holds significant value in an educational setting. Create a piece of technology that you believe would profoundly impact the classroom environment for both teachers and students.
