The Prompt:

In a world that feels increasingly interconnected yet distant at times, your challenge is to create a piece of technology that fosters meaningful connections. Whether it’s bridging the gap between generations, strengthening global communication, building communities, or improving access to critical resources, your innovation should aim to bring people closer together. Imagine technology that connects students with mentors, remote workers with their teams, or individuals with untapped opportunities. How can technology strengthen bonds, break down barriers, and create networks that transcend physical, social, or cultural divides? The power of connection is yours to explore—design a solution that makes the world feel just a little smaller, yet more united.
